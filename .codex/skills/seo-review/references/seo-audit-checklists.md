# SEO Audit Checklists

Use these checklists to score a page. Recommended total: 30 points.

## Title Tag (4 points)

- Length 50-60 characters
- Primary keyword appears early
- Unique and descriptive
- Communicates benefit or outcome

## Meta Description (4 points)

- Length 150-160 characters
- Starts with an action verb (Learn, Understand, Discover)
- Contains primary keyword
- Promises specific value or scope

## Keyword Placement (5 points)

- Primary keyword in title
- Primary keyword in meta description
- Primary keyword in first 100 words
- Keyword appears in at least one H2
- Copy reads naturally (no stuffing)

## Content Structure (6 points)

- Opening hook addresses the user question or pain
- Early example or illustration (first 200 words)
- Scannable sections with short paragraphs
- H2s map to user questions or tasks
- Key terms are highlighted on first mention
- Sufficient depth for intent (avoid thin content)

## Featured Snippet Optimization (4 points)

- 40-60 word definition for "What is" queries (when relevant)
- Question-form H2s (What/How/Why)
- Steps or numbered list for "How to" queries
- Table for comparisons or alternatives (when relevant)

## Internal Linking (4 points)

- 3-5 related internal links in body
- Descriptive anchor text
- Prerequisites called out (when relevant)
- Related/next-step section present

## Technical SEO (3 points)

- Single H1 per page
- Clean, readable slug with keyword
- Indexable (canonical, robots, sitemap, no broken links)

## Trust and Accuracy (non-scored, but recommended)

- Author, date, and sources where accuracy matters
- External references for claims or data
- Clear ownership of updates or versioning
